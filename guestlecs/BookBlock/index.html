The Talk

VENKATAKRISHNAN RANGANATHAN

Head of Social Media and Workplace Re-imagination Practice, TCS

The rapid proliferation of digital technologies is changing the nature of business, work and is also disrupting society as we know it. This talk will focus on the forces that will and are shaping the technology landscape in the coming years - mobile & pervasive, social, cloud, big data and AI/Machine learning and how we are headed towards several singularity moments in multiple industries in the next decade.
